Consulting

  

 

  

On-site consultants support is popular in pharmaceutical and biotech industry. Most pharmaceutical companies have identified preferred vendor companies to provide Biostatistician and SAS Programmer consultants.  Most of the times these preferred vendors do not have professionals as their own employee, they takes professionals from small vendor companies who are not preferred vendors of any companies. Taking consultants in 2-tier or 3-tier or even more tiers is a major concern to Consultant as their compensation is severely reduced as the number of tiers increases. Alpha Stats is against the suppression of professionals, and ensures that a consultant must receive reasonable compensation and benefits along with continuity in consulting assignments
